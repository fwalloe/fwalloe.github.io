I grew increasingly interested in information security in the years after Stuxnet and as an MA student at Goldsmiths University I wrote my dissertation about the Snooper's Charter.

By 2015 this interest had become sufficiently intense to justify a bachelor degree in Information Security at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology in Gjøvik (formerly Høyskolen i Gjøvik).

I work past-time as an analyst in Oslo and envision infosec becoming a full-time profession over time, though I plan to also combine it with sporatic journalism projects and articles as this is a beat that too few journalists cover adequately.
